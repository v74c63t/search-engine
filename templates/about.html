About

  • This is a search engine built from scratch that is capable of handling 10000s documents and web pages with a query response of under 300 ms
  • The inverted index is created by Porter Stemming the words that is parsed from the documents or webpages and assigning each term a weighted tf-idf score that will be used to rank the retrieval results
  • A word is given more weight or importance depending on its html tag
  • Implemented ranked retrieval that has stop word processing and term at a time retrieval
